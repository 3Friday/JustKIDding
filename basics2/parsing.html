You might not have heard the word "parsing" but you have done it.

Parsing is just reading and trying to understand. So it is a term from both linguistics (the science of language) and computer science.

Just like you string one word after the other to get the meaning of what is written.

Computer do the same but they do it in reverse order: from right to left.

Some languages parse the code all at once and other do it line by line when you tell them to execute that program.
