HTTP or Hypertext Transfer Protocol is the way that your computer communicates with the computer where the web page that you want to view is located.

In order to exchange information the client (that is your device) and the server (that is the machine that hosts a page that you try to view) send each other requests.

The two most common HTTP request methods are called GET and POST.

  • GET is just like saying "Site, please give me your sweet, precious data!"
  • POST is when you want the server to send you personalized page based on what you tell it to

Of course, usually GET is safer but it also shows you the same as for anyone else. And it may be faster because it doesn't have to process your data and put together a new page with just the right type of info especially for you. But alas, trade offs have to be made.
