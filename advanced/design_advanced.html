They say that 90% of design is typography. We already saw how to install fonts but now lets make it pretty.

    How to match the title fonts with the one in the body.

  • Typeface vs. font: art and its embodiment.
  • Serif vs. sans serif: usually we want one of each.
  • Font famalies: have a backup in case the browser hasn't downloaded the file or doesn't support it.
  • Font size, style/ weight and variant: bigger is better?; bold/ italic; uppercase /normal/ small caps.
  • Text align, decoration, indent and shadow: left/ center/ right; none, underline, overline, line-through, and inherit; how big of an indent and shadow.

Another important thing is colo(u)r: of the text, backgrounds. Also transparency. And patterns.

Spacing: blank spaces allow our mind and eyes to rest. Between paragraphs, between lines and between words.
