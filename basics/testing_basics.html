Once you make sure that your page is free of errors, you might want to check if it fulfills you needs. This is where testing comes into play.

If you use your page in conditions as close to real ones, how many issues do you encounter. Some ideas on what to looks for:

  • Is you page too slow? Can it be better? (Reliability and Performance)
  • Can a random person find easily what they are looking for? (Design)
  • Can you add or remove elements quickly? (Scalability)
  • Can your page be attacked and how to prevent it. (Security)
  • Is the page displayed correctly in each browser? (Compatibility)
  • Check your grammar and spelling! Is everything correct?
  • When you click on a link does it open the page that it is supposed to?
